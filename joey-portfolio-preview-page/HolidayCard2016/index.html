Faegre Baker Daniels is pleased to continue our tradition of giving back during this season. This year we celebrate organizations that share our passion for advancing women in business and in the legal profession. Learn more about past organizations we have supported.

We will make a $5/£4 donation to the organization of your choice and will donate up to $5,000 to each organization.

Why Organizations That Advance Women?

Women are less than 22 percent of partners and 18 percent of managing partners at American law firms (according to the ABA’s Commission on Women in the Profession) and only 4 percent of CEOs at Fortune 500 companies (according to Fortune).

Catalyst is the leading nonprofit organization accelerating progress for women through workplace inclusion. With operations in the United States, Canada, Europe, India, Australia, and Japan, and more than 800 supporting organizations, Catalyst is the trusted resource for research, information and advice about women at work.
Corporate Counsel Women of Color (CCWC) is a support network to in-house women of color that facilitates networking, promotes career advancement and the success of in-house women of color, and promotes global diversity in the legal profession and workplace. It includes more than 3,500 in-house women attorneys of color worldwide.
The Diversity & Flexibility Alliance is a think tank dedicated to creating inclusive cultures that leverage diversity and flexibility to attract and retain top talent. It builds opportunities that support the advancement of women and flexible work cultures through research, solutions and strategies to promote high performance environments. Alliance members cover over 30 percent of the AmLaw 100.
The National Association of Women Lawyers (NAWL) provides leadership, a collective voice and essential resources to advance women in the legal profession and advocate for the equality of women under the law. NAWL’s membership includes corporate legal departments, law firms, law schools and bar associations.
